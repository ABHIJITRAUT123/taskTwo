United Phosphorus Ltd. is the world’s third-largest agro- chemical brand whose main aim is to provide open access to agriculture networks around the globe. UPL offers best agricultural services like IOT and innovations in agriculture, farmer education, aquatic solution, pronutiva and much more for good plant cultivation. No limits, no borders. For UPL.

Challenge

Being in the chemical industry, gaining the trust of the consumer was the biggest challenge. To tackle this, we created specific websites in the native language of their location. Since, the target audience for UPL is majorly farmers & small business owners, we ensured that every website is pixel perfect and easy to scan for the users arriving on the website. As is the case, every website loads differently for every region. Managing the adaptiveness, responsiveness and the latency of the website was a mammoth mountain to climb. Another challenge was to make sure all the UPL sub- websites are laden with the latest technology and a team that is dedicatedly working to keep all the websites running and live.
